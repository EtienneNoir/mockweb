WHAT TO DO IF YOU ARE ARRESTED ?


BAIL

If the police arrest you, you can usually pay bail money and go home until the date of your court case. The amount depends on the severity of the alleged offence and the previous record of the detainee. You should inquire whether you can be released on warning, police bail or whether an after-hours prosecutor can determine a bail amount After the court hearing you will get your bail money back, even if you are found guilty, unless you break bail conditions.
